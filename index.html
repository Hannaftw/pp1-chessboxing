An introduction to chessboxing

What is it? A hybrid sport that combines alternating rounds of boxing and chess in the most glorious fashion.

Who would do such a thing? There are several amateur clubs but also official international competitions managed by The World Chessboxing Organization (WCBO). (the name of the international foundation, link new tab). To compete professionally a player needs to have both knowledge and experience of chess and be deemed physically fit enough for boxing.

How do you win? By knocking out your opponent in the boxing ring or defeating them on the chessboard, you do not have to win both events in order to win the match.

Can you picture it? The battle of wits and the intricate dance of the pieces on the board in the rounds of chess combined with the adrenaline-fueled exchanges in the ring during the sweat-inducing boxing rounds. It almost sounds too good to be true, but you are not dreaming! This is a real sport and it is here to stay.
