CHESS

- The art of mental combat

A defeat on the board may not hurt as much physically as being knocked out, but a checkmate may feel like a punch to the gut.

Mastering the board,
a chessboxing essential

Welcome to the cerebral realm of chess, an integral half of the exhilarating world of chessboxing. While the physical prowess of boxing is visible to the eye, the strategic depth of chess forms the unseen battleground where minds clash with equal intensity. This page is your gateway to understanding the foundational rules and strategies of chess. Whether you are new to the board or looking to sharpen your tactics, we have you covered.

Learn about the history of chess, the rules and and grasp the basic principles of the game, you will find what every chessboxer needs to command the board. Remember, in chessboxing, mental agility is as crucial as physical strength. It's not just about the checkmate; it's about outthinking your opponent in every way. Let's embark on this journey of mental mastery together. Prepare to engage your mind, one move at a time.

Hans Tikkanen, a chess grandmaster and five time Swedish champion (who also happens to be a friend of the creator of this site)
